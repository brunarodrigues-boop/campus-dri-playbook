Campus DRI Daily Workflow

Alpha Schools  ·  Quick Reference
Print & Keep
Every Morning  ·  15–30 min
1 Open TimeBack

Review your campus roster. Prioritize risk, not alphabetical order.

2 Look for Red Flags
  • Below 80% of expected XP
  • Below 80% of expected time
  • Below 80% learning accuracy (same subject)
  • Doom Loop (3+ failed attempts)
  • Previous intervention due for follow-up
One bad day ≠ intervention  ·  Three consecutive days = investigate
3 Investigate

Ask

  • Placement issue?
  • Knowledge gap?
  • Motivation?
  • Attendance?
  • Technical?
  • Rushing or guessing?

Look for

  • False Greens
  • Rushing
  • Guessing
  • Skipping explanations
  • Idling
  • Gaming XP
4 Decide

Can I fix this immediately?

✅ Yes
  • Message guide
  • Adjust plan
  • Schedule coaching
  • Document
❌ No
  • Collect evidence
  • Escalate to correct team
  • Track until resolved
5 Communicate

Every guide response must cover:

✔ What I found
✔ Why it's happening
✔ What action is needed
✔ When I'll follow up
6 Document
  • If you acted — log it
  • If you escalated — log it
  • If you followed up — log it
If it isn't documented, it didn't happen.
Weekly Check

For every student: Are they on track for their next milestone?

Yes ✓
  • Continue monitoring
  • Look for acceleration opportunities
No ✗
  • Find root cause
  • Adjust the plan
  • Notify the guide
  • Set review date
Two unsuccessful weeks → Deep Dive

Think Like a Campus DRI
  • Investigate before advising.
  • Look for patterns, not isolated events.
  • Treat root causes, not symptoms.
  • Coach guides — don't replace them.
  • Verify before escalating.
  • Document everything.
  • Own the outcome until it is resolved.
